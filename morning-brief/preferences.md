## Sections to Include

2. Today's calendar
3. Top emails needing attention

5. Stocks
6. Heads up

## Email Filtering
- Only consider Gmail `category:primary`.
- Ignore emails that are already read.
- Time window: unread Primary emails from the last 24 hours by default.
- Also include unread important Primary emails from the last 48 hours.
- Skip: marketing, newsletters, promos, automated notifications, calendar invites, receipts unless obviously urgent
- Prioritize: wife, manager, daycare/school, doctors, tenants/rental property, bills/deadlines, travel, family logistics
- Cap: top 5 by importance
- Collapse duplicate emails about the same underlying topic.
- Do not repeat emails already included in a previous day's brief.
- Output sender + subject only, not long summaries
- Include a count of attention-worthy emails
- Never include full email bodies in the final brief or logs

## Calendar Filtering
- Include all events today
- Skip declined events
- 12-hour time format
- Include time and title only unless location is clearly useful
- Flag especially early events, travel time, kid/family logistics, medical/school/daycare items, or unusual conflicts

## Stocks
